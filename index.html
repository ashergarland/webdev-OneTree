What we do?

Take a look at what we do as a company

We plant one tree for every product we sell. With your help, this program will secure the biodiversity of our forests and ensure our future generations of the adventure and exploration we are so lucky to have today.

One tree can have an incredible impact on the ecosystems we plant in. All trees planted are local species, often times in some of the harshest regions. We aim to plant in areas that have the greatest need.

It takes about one tree to make 3,000 units of our varying products. This means we are replenishing our forests at a rate of roughly 3,000 times our tree usage. This makes a vast impact on the sustainability of the forests.
